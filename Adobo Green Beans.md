# Adobo Green Beans

## Ingredients & Instructions

- 2 shallots
- 1/2 lb pork sausage
- oil

Slice the shallots thin and fry them in oil in a large frying pan. Once they are
soft or starting to brown, add the sausage.  Cook until golden brown.

- 1 lb long green beans
- 2 cloves garlic
- 1 tsp black pepper
- 1/4 C soy sauce
- 1/4 C white vinegar

Chop the green beans into 2-3 inch segments. Mince the garlic. Add these, ground
black pepper, soy sauce, and vinegar to the frying pan.  Cover and simmer for 15
to 20 minutes.

- soy sauce
- aji mirin
- 4 cloves garlic
- 1 tsp vinegar

Season to taste with mirin and soy sauce.  Mince the garlic.  Turn off the heat,
then stir in the garlic and the final vinegar.

- rice

Serve over rice.


## Notes

This was  an attempt to recreate  a more traditional Filipino adobo  (which used
pork belly and fried garlic)  on short notice;  I ended up liking it better than
the original.  I used Jimmy Dean's "hot" sausage for the pork,  and Shanxi black
vinegar for the finishing vinegar.
