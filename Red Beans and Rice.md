# Red Beans and Rice

Makes 6 servings.


## Ingredients & Instructions

- 1 lb dried red kidney beans

Soak the kidney beans overnight, then drain.

- 1 lb fully cooked smoked sausage (e.g., kielbasa), chopped
- 1 large onion, chopped
- 1 cup celery with leaves, chopped
- 5 cloves garlic, chopped

Sauté sausage in a Dutch oven over medium heat until brown, about 5 minutes. Add
onion,  celery,  and garlic to the pot and sauté until the onion is translucent,
about another 5 minutes.

- 3/4 tsp garlic powder
- 1/2 tsp ground allspice
- 1/2 tsp ground cloves
- 1/2 tsp ground white pepper
- 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
- 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper

Add the powdered spices.

- 8 C water or broth
- 4 bay leaves
- 1 T dried thyme
- 2 tsp dried oregano
- 1 tsp hot pepper sauce (e.g., Tabasco)

Add the drained beans with new water or broth, then add the herbs and hot sauce.
Bring to a boil,  then reduce heat and simmer until the beans are tender and the
mixture is thick, about 3 hours.  Stir frequently and add water as needed.

- salt and pepper
- cooked white rice

Season with salt and pepper.  Serve over hot white rice.
