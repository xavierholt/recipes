# Roasted Fennel

## Ingredients & Instructions

- 2 large fennel bulbs

Preheat oven to 400 F.  Slice the fennel into 1/2-inch slabs, and lay these on a
baking sheet.

- 3 T olive oil
- 2 cloves garlic
- 1 tsp dried thyme

Mince the garlic.  Mix the garlic and thyme into the olive oil,  then brush this
mixture over the fennel.

- 3/4 tsp salt
- 1 tsp pepper

Sprinkle with salt and pepper.  Roast for 25 minutes.

- 1/4 C parmesan

Sprinkle with shredded parmesan, then roast for another ten minutes (or more, if
the fennel isn't as tender as you like it).  Serve warm.


## Notes

Based on [this recipe](https://www.everylastbite.com/roasted-fennel/).
